Water Solenoid

The water solenoid of our washing machine got stuck-open. Therefore the water from the inlet does not stop from flowing even if the tub is overflowing with water. I inspected the solenoid and found out that it is stuck. I then went online and ordered a new one. Two days later, it arrived and I installed it immediately. So far it works just fine.

Worn Intake Manifold

I replaced a 13-year-old cracked intake manifold of my dad's motorcycle. Since it was cracked, the fuel from the carburetor cannot enter the combustion chamber because it has higher resistance in terms of flow than the cracked surfaces in the intake manifold. I installed a new one and tuned the carburetor manually by ear.
